My Story

I'm Amir Tamang, a Software Engineer and Data Analyst with a unique professional background. My technical journey is anchored by a BSc in Computing and my current pursuit of a Master of Information Technology at Australian Catholic University (ACU) here in Sydney.

Unlike traditional paths, my experience bridges high-level technical architecture and high-pressure operational environments. I have built containerized e-commerce systems, trained computer vision scripts, and analyzed complex graph databases. At the same time, my work in fast-paced Australian logistics and hospitality sectors—using enterprise tools like SAP Business Warehouse—has taught me the critical importance of resilience, rigorous quality assurance, and solving problems in real-time.

I believe the best software doesn't just run efficiently; it fundamentally improves operational workflows. I am passionate about leveraging my dual expertise to build systems that are robust, user-centric, and data-driven.
